## Tips:
- Keep backgrounds transparent or very light where noted
- Use these exact purple tones: #4A2F6B (dark), #9D8EC2 (lavender), #B8B5C9 (silver)
- Simple illustration style works better than photorealistic
- NO GOLD colors anywhere!
